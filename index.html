Glossary

Basic Indicators and Metrics

RSI
Oscillator measuring trend strength (overbought/oversold).
MACD
Moving average convergence divergence based on differences between MAs.
SMA
Simple moving average; a basic MA.
EMA
Exponential moving average; weighted MA.
MA
General moving average; basis for MACD and SMA.
CAGR
Compound annual growth rate.
Sharpe Ratio
Return relative to volatility risk.
Sortino Ratio
Sharpe variant considering downside risk only.
Max Drawdown
Maximum capital drop from a peak.

Risk & Money Management

Kelly Sizing
Optimal position sizing formula.
Stop Loss
Order limiting loss; see also Trailing Stop.
Trailing Stop
Stop loss that follows price movement.
Take Profit
Automatic order to lock gains.
Trailing Take Profit
Take profit level moving with price.
Leverage
Borrowed exposure multiplier.
Martingale
Doubling stake after losses.
Delta Neutral
Hedged position with no directional exposure.
Hedging
Risk reduction by offsetting positions.

Instruments & Markets

Spot Market
Immediate asset exchange.
Perpetual Futures
Futures without expiry; tied to funding rate.
Options
Right to buy or sell in future.
ETFs
Exchange-traded funds holding baskets of assets.
Long Position
Bet on price increase.
Short Position
Bet on price decrease.

Quant & ML in Trading

OHLCV
Candlestick data: open, high, low, close, volume.
LSTM
Recurrent neural network for sequences.
Random Forest
Ensemble of decision trees.
XGBoost
Gradient boosting method.
Footprint / CVD
Volume and order flow analysis.

Trading Styles

Scalping
Fast trades for small profits.
Swing Trading
Holding positions for days or weeks.
Day Trading
Closing positions within the day.
Position Trading
Long-term investing.

Bybit/Binance Terms

Funding Rate
Payment aligning perpetual futures price with spot.
Margin
Collateral securing leveraged trades.
PnL
Profit and loss, realized or unrealized.
Liquidation
Forced closing due to insufficient margin.
